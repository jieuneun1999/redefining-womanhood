SBECED 1D – Redefining Womanhood

We are SBECED 1D from Quezon City University, a collective of aspiring educators committed to creativity, collaboration, and social awareness.

Our project, “Redefining Womanhood,” challenges traditional stereotypes and celebrates the evolving strength, intelligence, resilience, and diversity of women in modern society.

Our exhibit panel symbolizes transformation—just as discarded materials can be reshaped into meaningful art, perspectives about women can also be reconstructed into narratives of empowerment and equality.

This website serves as the digital extension of our exhibit, expanding advocacy beyond the classroom.
